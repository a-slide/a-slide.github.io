About me

In brief

I am a computational biologist currently working as a senior postdoc at the EMBL-EBI in Ewan Birney's Team. I have a dual background in molecular biology and bioinformatics. My PhD was at the bench in virology and epigenetics at the INSERM 1089 (University of Nantes, France). I had the opportunity to setup my own team in my PhD lab as a junior team leader and to obtain a master's degree in bioinformatics. I then moved to the EMBL-EBI to expend my skills in data analysis and software development. I have been working on a broad range of topics including RNA modifications in cancer and more recently medaka fish functional genomics.

Have a look at my online profiles to know more about my academic track record or have a quick look at my top publications.
