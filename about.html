About me

I am a computational biologist currently working as a senior postdoc at the EMBL-EBI in Ewan Birney's Team. I have a dual background in molecular biology and bioinformatics. My PhD was at the bench in virology and epigenetics at the INSERM 1089 (University of Nantes, France). After my PhD, I was promoted to a junior team leader position and obtained an additional master's degree in bioinformatics, while developing expertise in sequencing methods related to viral vector genomes. I then moved to the EMBL-EBI to expand my skills in data analysis and software development. At the EBI, I have been working on a broad range of topics including RNA modifications in cancer and more recently medaka fish functional genomics, developing a deep knowledge of nanopore sequencing.

During my career, I have contributed to over 20 scientific publications and communications.
